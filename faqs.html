Frequently Asked Questions

Will the clear end of Highlater remove any brand of highlighter?

No, unfortunately our clear side is only engineered to remove our Highlater product. We are currently in the process of designing a product that removes all brands of highlighters but for the time being, we can only remove our Highlater brand.

Will the Highlater fade over time?

No. The Highlater product only fades away once the clear end is applied. If the clear coat is not applied, the product will last forever. See our features page for more details behind our technology.

Is the ink toxic?

No. We use a non-toxic washable ink so that it is safe for children! Our ink will also not stain clothes or skin, and can be removed with mild soap and cold water.

How long will the Highlater product last?

Our Highlater product will have the same lifespan as any other highlighter marker. The average highlater marker lasts for 6-8 months with everyday use (about two college terms).

What happens if my Highlater runs out of one side,but not the other?

Our Highlater product is designed to have the same exact amounts of the highlighter color and the clear coat. Our goal is to provide the equal ratio so that all highlighting can be erased. For some reason if this is not the case, please visit our Contact Us page and let us know! We will send you a new product in order to live up to our products gaurentee.
