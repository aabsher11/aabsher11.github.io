Meet Our Team

Shannon Meads

Marketing and Promotions Director

Shannon has been with Highlater since 2011, after she graduated from Willamette University with a degree in marketing. Shannon has been a valuable asset to the company and is responsible for all of our ad campaigns and marketing strategies. She even created our first commercial, which will be released in June 2015. In her free time, Shannon loves to bake and take black and white photos.

Greg Lawson

Product Developer

Greg is the brains behind our whole operation and the idea man behind Highlater. As a student at Willamette University, he was unable to re-sell his textbooks because he highlighted in them. In order to solve this dilemma, Greg invented Highlater. Now, Greg oversees all product development. In his free time, Greg enjoys playing videogames and graphic design.

Jordan Yellowstone

Business Operations Coordinator

Jordan handles all of the finances of the business. Jordan serves as our company accountant and chief budget officer. Anything that involves money, Jordan is in charge! In her free time, Jordan loves to hike and eat pizza
