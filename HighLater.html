FAQ's

Since the ink is removable, Will it fade over time?

No. The highlater ink will only fade when the second end is applied to the highlighter ink. If the second end is not applied, the ink will be permanent.

Is the ink washable? Will it stain clothes?

No. The ink is waterproof, but removable with mild soap.
