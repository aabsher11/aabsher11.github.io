Product Features

Why you need us!

Our highlaters are one of a kind. We understand being a poor college student, having to spend hundreds of dollars on books and not being able to highlight in them because you need to resell them at the end of the semester. Or worse, we have also experienced that first semester when you highlight in your books, and then are unable to sell them back. Then you are stuck with a 200 textbook and are forced to eat ramen for the rest of the semester. Don't be that student. Instead use us! Highlater is a state of the art highlighter that allows for previous highlights to be removed.

How we work

Simply highlight with the colored side as you normally would. After your semester is over, simply re-highlight over what you have highlighted with the clear side. Wait 24 hours. Then, all of your previous highlights will be removed and the re-sell value will reappear. Yes, it really is that simple.
